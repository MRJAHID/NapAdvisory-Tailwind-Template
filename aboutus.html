OUR STORY

Our NAP Partner Story

Founded in 2024, NAP was established with the goal of providing personalized and reliable tax and VAT advisory services to businesses of all sizes. Our founders, MD Nassim, Alamin & Pritam Das recognized the need for a firm that combines deep technical expertise with a commitment to exceptional client service. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted partner for businesses across various industries, known for our proactive approach and proven results. At NAP, our mission is to empower businesses with expert tax and VAT advisory services that ensure compliance, optimize financial strategies, and drive growth. We are dedicated to helping our clients navigate the complex world of tax regulations with confidence and clarity.
