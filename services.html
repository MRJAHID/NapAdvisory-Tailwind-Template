Income TAX- Individual Income Tax

Individual income tax is a tax imposed by governments on the income generated by individuals :

Income tax is a vital component of a country's revenue system, enabling governments to fund essential public services and infrastructure projects. It is typically levied on individuals and businesses based on their earnings, with rates often varying according to income levels. Progressive tax systems aim to ensure that those with higher incomes contribute a larger percentage, promoting economic equity. Accurate filing and compliance are crucial, as tax evasion can result in significant penalties.
